MORE ABOUT WHAT I DO

Performances

I am a percussionist performing in two a cappella groups in the greater Boston area:

Pitch Slapped (Berklee)
The Unisons (Northeastern)

It's an honor to be able to rehearse and perform with both of these wonderful groups, and I've learned so much from my time making music with them.

I plan to continue performing my music live for as long as possible, while recording tracks in the mean time.

Arrangements

I create arrangements of funk and r&b music. I use vocals in the style of contemporary a cappella in my arrangements, as well as techniques from solo beatboxing to emphasize moments and flesh out the sound of a song.

You can find my arrangements on...

Instagram, YouTube, and (coming soon) Spotify.

Recordings

I record all of my arrangement productions myself. I use a combination of Ableton Live, DaVinci Resolve, Blender, and Inkscape for audiovisual production. I am self-taught in all of these programs, and I'm more than happy to share my process if asked.

If you are a videographer, editor, or artist and you like my work, please reach out! I am very open to collaborating to create visuals for my music.

Lessons

I have taught several people one-on-one over the years, and I have held lessons and a masterclass at Boston Sings (BOSS) 2025.

If you are interested in learning more about vocal percussion technique, arrangment, production, or developing your own style, pelase fill out my lesson form! I try to keep rates low so lessons are more accessible, since I really love sharing this knowledge.
